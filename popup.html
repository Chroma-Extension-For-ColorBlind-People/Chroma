Chroma

Chroma is a chrome extension that will help color blind people distinguish between the colors they cannot distinguish because of their color vision deficiency. We have worked to cater the color vision deficiency for

  • Protonopia
  • Deutronopia
  • Tritanopia

If you want to access the Chroma - Exetension for Color Deficiency Aid, Login In to your existing account.

You have Protonopia, which is color deficiency to differentiate red shades, then you are not able to see the red shades properly. So, we have applied filters on all the elements of red color or their combinations with green and blue color which protonopia affected people are unable to distinguish from real shades. The details of these shades are as follow:

  • Red: Red Only RGB(R,0,0)
  • Red & Green: Red and Green Combination: Brown and Yellow Shades RGB(R,G,0)
  • Green: Green Only RGB(0,G,0)
  • Red & Blue: Red and Blue Combination: Purple and Pink Shades RGB(R,0,B)
  • Green & Blue: Blue and Green Combination: Teal, Light greenish blue shades RGB(0,G,B)
  • Red, Green & Blue: Red, Green and Blue Combination:Red, Blue and Green combinations that are not distinguishable. RGB(R,G,B)
